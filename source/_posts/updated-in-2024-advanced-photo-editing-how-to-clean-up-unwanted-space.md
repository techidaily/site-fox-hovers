## Part 2: How to Blur Photo Background?

Step 1: Isolate Subject

Isolate your subject on a separate layer by accessing the ‚ÄúQuick Selection‚Äù tool and clicking on the ‚ÄúSelect Subject‚Äù button. You can use the "Lasso" tool to refine your selection. Hold the ‚ÄúCTRL‚Äù key and select your layer mask. Go to the "Select" tab, find and expand "Modify," and tap "Expand." Select a suitable number and tap ‚ÄúOK‚Äù Next, go to the ‚ÄúEdit‚Äù tab and select ‚ÄúContent-Aware Fill."

You can change the field lines to adjust the depth of field for your image and create a smooth transition. You can also control the intensity of the blur from the slider present on the right.
